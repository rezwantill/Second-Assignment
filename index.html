BMI Calculator

The Body Mass Index (BMI) Calculator can be used to calculate BMI value and corresponding weight status while taking age into consideration.BMI is a measurement of a person's leanness or corpulence based on their height and weight, and is intended to quantify tissue mass.It is widely used as a general indicator of whether a person has healthy body weight for their height.Specifically, the value obtained from the calculation of BMI is used to categorize whether a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese depending on what range the value falls between.
